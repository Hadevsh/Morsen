Morse Code Spacing

• dit: 1 unit

− dah: 3 units

Space between letters in a word is three times the duration of a dit, equivalent to the length of a dah.

Between words, the space is seven times the duration of a dit.

However, here in straight key mode the duration of the space between characters is 1200ms not 3 units (proportional to WPM) for easier use.
